Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Objective

Animal Crossings is a franchise based game.
You need to upgrade your town and home, and pay off debts.
You start at a deserted island and are greeted by Tanukis (or racoons in the west) named the Nooks.
Then you go from there, making your own decisions on how to epxand your island.

Characters


Here are some of my favorite characters in Animal Crossing: NHR.
They are called villagers, and you can "collect" them and add them to your island.
They have different personalities and do different activities. There are animals and humanoids alike.

Roblox

Roblox is an interactive live platform with many,many games to play. It has a user base of about 200 million people.
You can friend and follow users. create your own game using roblox studio, and customize your avatar.
You can also send messages to users in your friends list, or, if your settings allow it, people you follow.


Although it is a fun enriching game, there are multiple faults that could occur.
You could be hacked and your account password compromised
You could also be scammed by websites claiming to provide free "Robux", the games currency which you need to pay real money for. With it, you may buy virtual items.
In order to prevent this, go to this website to learn more about digital saftey on roblox.
